Category:WR

~How many WRs do you have?~

1:WR
WR stages number(a)(ex.Alpha_0910 has '19' WR stages)
Points(a*10)=A (ex.Alpha_0910 gets 190 points)
2:Tied WR
Tied WR stages number(b)(ex.Alpha_0910 has '14' Tied WR stages)
Points(b*1)=B (ex.Alpha_0910 gets 14 points)
3:Only One WR
Only One WR stages number(c)(ex.Alpha_0910 has '4' Only One WR stages)
Points(c*5)=C (ex.Alpha_0910 gets 20 points)
4:First WR
First WR stages number(d)(ex.Alpha_0910 has '5' First WR stages)
Points(d*3)=D (ex.Alpha_0910 gets 15 points)
Sum of A to D =Someone's points(ex.Alpha_0910 gets 239 points)
